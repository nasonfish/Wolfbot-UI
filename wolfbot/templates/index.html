#Werewolf IRC Channel

#Werewolf is a friendly IRC channel on EsperNet for playing "Werewolf", a theme of Mafia, via an online bot that manages the game (Wolfbot).

The bot is an updated version of freenode's lykos Werewolf bot. It was modified to fit EsperNet's playing needs, and is constantly updated by nasonfish and Vgr.
